My Story

I am Vaibhav Gupta, a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student at Pranveer Singh Institute
of Technology, Kanpur. My expertise spans Python, C, C++, Java, machine learning, operating systems,
and DBMS. I have a proven track record of converting theoretical knowledge into tangible solutions, with

development of a personalized product recommendation system for Flipkart Grid. My passion for innovation
and problem-solving drives my pursuit of a dynamic career in computer science and engineering. I am interested
in exploring the fields of Software Engineering, Data Science, and Machine Learning, and I am open to opportunities
for the same.
